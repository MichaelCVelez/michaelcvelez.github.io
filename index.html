About Me

Hello there!

My name is Michael and I am an aspiring professional software and game developer.

Born and raised in El Paso TX, I developed an interest in all aspects of modern technology through my early passion for gaming. I graduated from the University of Texas at El Paso with my B.S. in Computer Science at the young age of 20, thanks to my strong perseverance and enrollment in an Early College High School.

Since obtaining my degree, I have earned diverse working experience in the healthcare, entertainment media, semiconductor manufacturing and gaming industries. The highlights of my career have been working with companies I grew up admiring including IGN and Blizzard Entertainment.

In my spare time I enjoy photography, cosplay, writing horror stories, creating mods, keeping up with a strict fitness routine and streaming gameplay with my friends. If you can talk about Star Wars, Game of Thrones or Dragonball for hours on end, then we are likely off to a great start!

My hopes are that this website becomes a centralized hub for getting to know me and my interests, proudly displaying my accomplishments and broadening my connections. Thank you for stopping by and let's start creating something together!
